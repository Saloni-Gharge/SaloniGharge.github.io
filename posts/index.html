Quranntino: Indoor Positioning and Geofencing System for Covid Hospitals

Overview Quranntino is an innovative Indoor Positioning and Geofencing System designed to monitor Covid-19 patients within hospital premises. The system utilizes Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) bands and strategically placed receivers to ensure real-time tracking of patients. If a patient attempts to breach the geofence or remove the band, an instant alert is sent to hospital authorities via a web application. This solution was developed to support frontline healthcare workers by improving patient monitoring, security, and resource allocation during the pandemic.
